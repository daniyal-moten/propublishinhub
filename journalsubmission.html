Journal Submission

Our journal submission service has experts who are skilled in submitting papers to various international journals. When your manuscript is ready for submission, let us complete this tedious process on your behalf.

Our Journal Submission Process

  • Upon receiving payment, we will send a confirmation email with your assignment details
  • We will prepare a submission checklist based on your target journal requirements
  • We will also send all the necessary forms (such as authorship, copyright transfer form, etc.) on which your signatures are required, if any
  • Upon receiving all the necessary information from you, we complete the journal submission process—online or offline

Frequently Asked Questions

Our standard journal submission service takes 3 business days to complete. This includes preparing your manuscript according to journal guidelines, completing all necessary forms, and submitting your paper to the target journal. For urgent submissions, please contact our support team for expedited service options.

You'll need to provide your complete manuscript, any supplementary materials, author information, and any specific forms required by the target journal. Our team will guide you through the exact requirements for your chosen journal and help prepare all necessary documentation for submission.

After submission, you'll receive a submission report and confirmation from the journal. The journal will then begin their review process. We'll keep you informed about the status of your submission and help you address any queries from the journal. If revisions are requested, our team can assist you with preparing the revised manuscript for resubmission.
