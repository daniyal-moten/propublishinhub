Manuscript Plagiarism Check

When you submit a manuscript to any reputable journal it will be scrutinized for plagiarized content. Plagiarism, intentional or otherwise, is viewed as a violation of ethics and, if detected in your paper, leads to automatic rejection. In addition, plagiarism can tarnish your reputation and credibility as an author. To avoid such situations, Propublicationhub's Advanced Plagiarism Check service helps you identify areas in your manuscript that might be flagged as plagiarism. Using advanced plagiarism software, we scan and check the originality of your copy. We then provide you a report which highlights any such instances.

  • Revised Manuscript ready for re-submission or rapid submission to a new journal
  • Revised/Rejected Paper Editing Report
  • Tailor-made Cover Letter
  • Certificate of Editing (if requested)
